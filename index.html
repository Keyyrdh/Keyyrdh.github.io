About

I split my time between close reading and close debugging. My primary work is in the study of texts, sources, and the methods used to interpret them carefully — and alongside that, I've been teaching myself to build things: small scripts, computer-vision experiments, and, apparently, this website.

I like projects that force me to slow down and get the fundamentals right, whether that's tracing a chain of transmission or tracing why a library won't import.

"Careful reading and careful code come from the same habit — checking your sources before you trust the output."
