// 01 — About

Hi, I'm Keysha Radhitya.

I was born in Lampung, Indonesia, though Karawang, West Java is where I truly grew up — about 14 years there, from kindergarten through junior high. I finished high school in Purwakarta before starting a new chapter abroad.

Today I'm an undergraduate student at Al-Azhar University in Cairo, Egypt. Studying abroad has taught me independence, adaptability, and the value of embracing different cultures and perspectives.

Technology has always been one of my biggest interests — I'm fascinated by gadgets, digital products, and how quickly things evolve. Creativity is a big part of who I am too: I have experience in graphic design, photo editing, and video editing, and I enjoy making visuals that are both aesthetically pleasing and clear in what they communicate.

Outside of study, I recharge through cooking, basketball, swimming, traveling, and journaling. I believe growth comes from curiosity, consistency, and a positive mindset — and I'm always up for meeting new people and collaborating on interesting ideas.
